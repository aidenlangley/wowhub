    An upcoming recovery noho at Rawhitiroa, Ohaeawai

    posted 23/08/2021, 12:00:00

Whakaoranga Whanau Recovery Hub & He Waka Eke Noa te Tai Tokerau is a kaupapa Māori AOD community support service in Kaikohe. We're a team of passionate individuals who have lived experience.

We work together and collaborate with other health providers to strengthen, educate and empower individuals to reach their full potential.

You can find us at 135 Broadway, Kaikohe, Monday to Friday, 10am until 3pm.

You can contact peer support, tātou kaimanāki, at support@wowhub.co.nz, and for Freedom Whare related queries, you can email info@freedomwhare.co.nz.

E hara taku toa i te toa takitahi, engari he toa takitini

"My strength is not that of a single warrior, but that of many"

A favourite whakatauki of ours that embodies our ideals perfectly. A strong individual empowers us all.

We take a holistic approach when it comes to treatment. Behind the individual there's roopu, whanau, there's hinengaro, tinana and wairua. Addiction only accounts for a fraction of the recovery process.

When Taumarere is in need, Hokianga will answer the call, and vice versa.
