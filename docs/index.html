Planting the seed of hope...

Sharing gifts of recovery.

Whakaoranga Whanau Recovery Hub is a kaupapa Māori AOD community support service in Kaikohe. We're a team of passionate individuals who have lived experience.

We work together and collaborate with other health providers to strengthen, educate and empower individuals to reach their full potential.

You can find us at 135 Broadway, Kaikohe, Monday to Friday, 10am until 3pm.

"My strength is not that of a single warrior, but that of many"

A favourite whakatauki of ours that embodies our ideals perfectly. A strong individual empowers us all.

We take a holistic approach when it comes to treatment. Behind the individual there's roopu, whanau, there's hinganaro, tinana and wairua. Addiction only accounts for a fraction of the recovery process.

When Taumarere is in need, Hokianga will answer the call, and vice versa.
